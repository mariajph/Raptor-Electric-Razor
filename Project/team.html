Our story

We founded the company in 2002 with an initial motive to produce men's product line which includes shaving set, shaving creams and so on. In 2009 we narrowed our production to electric shavers so that we could focus on the developing electric shavers with latest and the leading technology. In 2022 we are spread across Canada with almost 27 warehouses and 17 manufacturing units.

We have manufacturing units spread across many provinces in Canada which include Ontario, British Columbia and so on.
